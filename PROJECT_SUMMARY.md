## 📚 Customization Quick Start

### Add Studies (In Key Studies)
```html
<div class="study-card">
    <div class="study-header">
        <h4>Study: [Title]</h4>
        <span class="study-year">[Year]</span>
    </div>
    <div class="study-content">
        <div class="study-detail">
            <strong>Research Question:</strong>
            <p>[Your text]</p>
        </div>
        <!-- Add more details -->
    </div>
</div>
```

### Update Author Info (In About)
- Find "Author Information" section
- Replace placeholders with your name, school, grade
- Add your contact email
- List relevant coursework and experience

### Change Colors (In CSS)
- Open `style.css`
- Find `:root { }` at the top
- Update color values:
  - `--color-primary: #0066cc;` (blue)
  - `--color-secondary: #00a8a8;` (teal)
  - Others as desired
