How to Add More Studies

This page is designed as a growing research portfolio. To add studies:

  1. Find the study in PubMed or journal databases
  2. Copy the study title and publication year
  3. Identify the research question, design, findings, and limitations
  4. Paste the PubMed or journal link into the citation field
  5. Add 1–2 sentence interpretation of clinical significance

Pro tip: Focus on studies from peer-reviewed journals (Nature, Lancet, JAMA, Cancer Cell, Clinical Chemistry, etc.) and clinical trials listed on ClinicalTrials.gov. Studies with large sample sizes, rigorous designs, and relevant cancer types will be most valuable.
