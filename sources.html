All claims on this site are traceable to peer-reviewed literature. Sources are organized by topic and formatted for easy verification.

About These Sources

This portfolio synthesizes evidence from PubMed-indexed journals, NCI publications, and ClinicalTrials.gov. Sources were selected for methodological quality, relevance, and recency. Where studies conflict, both are cited with context.

  • National Cancer Institute (NCI) (cancer.gov)

  • Journals: Science, NEJM, Lancet, Annals of Oncology, Nature Medicine, JAMA Oncology, Cancer Cell, Clinical Chemistry
